In Our Skin is a Multi-award winning animation short featured in the 2018 Cannes Festival by animator Rosa Beiroa. Music & Sound design by Susanne Aubert.

It's a film about the beauty of the female body. Through the freckles on the protagonistâ€™s back the viewer is taken into a world of morphing figures. The simplicity of the line drawing brings us closer to almost abstract forms while describing shapes, volumes of the body and folds of the skin.

In Our Skin took Rosa and I on such a journey we never could have anticipated! We are so happy it was well received, thank you to everyone who liked and shared!
