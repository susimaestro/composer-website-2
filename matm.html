Man and
the Mountain

The aim of 'Man and the Mountain - A Dolomiti Timelapse' is to raise awareness about the fragility of the Italian Dolomites and inspire potential mountain visitors to properly educate themselves about mountain etiquette and culture. The whole movie was shot over 3 months travelling in the Italian Dolomites but man's involvement in the Italian Dolomites spans centuries. As we enter an era whereby international cheap travel is possible are we pushing this delicate area beyond it's limits.
